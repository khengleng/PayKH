# Data Processing Addendum (Template — not legal advice)

> Starting template for B2B customers acting as data controllers.

- **Roles:** the Merchant is the controller of its customers' personal data;
  PayKH is a processor acting on documented instructions.
- **Sub-processors:** Railway (hosting/DB), Resend (email), Sentry (error
  tracking). We maintain a current list and give notice of changes.
- **Security measures:** per [security.md](../security.md) and the
  [threat model](../threat-model.md).
- **Sub-processing:** flow-down of equivalent obligations.
- **Assistance:** we assist with data-subject requests, DPIAs, and breach
  notification within contractual timelines.
- **Deletion/return:** on termination, data is deleted or returned per the
  retention policy.
- **International transfers:** governed by applicable Cambodian and partner-bank
  requirements.
