# Privacy Policy (Template — not legal advice)

> Starting template. Have counsel review before production use.

- **Data we process:** merchant account data (email, name, org), payment
  metadata, and (for merchants' customers) transaction references. We do **not**
  store customer banking credentials.
- **Purpose:** to provide payment processing, fraud prevention, billing, and
  support.
- **Security:** encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest for sensitive secrets
  (AES-256-GCM); hashed API keys/passwords; access controls + audit logs. See
  [security.md](../security.md).
- **Retention:** operational data retained while your account is active and per
  legal/accounting requirements; backups per our DR policy.
- **Sharing:** with Bakong/NBC and sub-processors (hosting: Railway; email:
  Resend; error tracking: Sentry) strictly to provide the service.
- **Your rights:** access, correction, export, and deletion requests to
  privacy@cambobia.com, subject to legal retention.
- **Breach:** we follow the [key-compromise](../runbooks/key-compromise.md) and
  incident runbooks and will notify affected parties as required by law.
